## Features

### 1. Text Chat
Simply type your question and press **Enter** or click the **Send** button.

**Example questions:**
- "How do I renew my passport?"
- "What documents are needed for OCI?"
- "पासपोर्ट कैसे बनवाएं?" (Hindi)

### 2. Voice Input 🎤
1. Click the **green microphone** button
2. Speak your question clearly
3. Click again to stop recording
4. Your speech will be converted to text automatically

**Supported languages for voice:**
All 22 Indian official languages + 11 South African languages

### 3. Document Scanning 📸
1. Click the **camera** button
2. Allow camera access when prompted
3. Position your document within the frame
4. Click **Capture Document**
5. The bot will extract information automatically

**Supported documents:**
- Passport (front page)
- Visa stamps
- OCI cards
- Aadhaar cards
- Birth certificates

### 4. File Upload 📄
1. Click the **orange file** button
2. Select a file from your device
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
4. Maximum size: 10MB

## Troubleshooting

### Bot Not Responding?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Wait 30 seconds and try again

### Voice Not Working?
1. Allow microphone access in browser
2. Check if microphone is connected
3. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

### Camera Not Working?
1. Allow camera access in browser
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Position document flat and steady

### Still Having Issues?
Contact support: support@sevasetu.gov.in
